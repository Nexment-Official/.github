# Nexment Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Nexment is committed to building a welcoming, respectful, and professional community for everyone.

Whether you're reporting bugs, suggesting ideas, improving documentation, testing projects, or participating in discussions, your contributions help make the ecosystem better.

We expect every community member to contribute positively and help create an environment where everyone feels respected.

---

## Our Principles

As a member of the Nexment community, you agree to:

- Treat everyone with respect and professionalism.
- Welcome constructive feedback and different perspectives.
- Focus discussions on ideas rather than individuals.
- Help create a friendly and inclusive community.
- Respect the time and effort of maintainers and other contributors.
- Follow the rules and guidelines of each Nexment project.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, insults, or abusive language.
- Trolling or intentionally disrupting discussions.
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to Nexment.
- Sharing private or confidential information without permission.
- Misleading, deceptive, or malicious behavior.
- Repeatedly ignoring community guidelines or maintainer decisions.

---

## AI-Generated Content

AI-assisted content is welcome, including documentation, bug reports, feature suggestions, and pull requests.

However, you are responsible for reviewing any AI-generated content before submitting it. Low-quality, inaccurate, or spam-like submissions may be closed or removed.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all official Nexment community spaces, including but not limited to:

- GitHub repositories
- GitHub Discussions
- The Nexment website
- Official social media accounts
- Any future community platforms managed by Nexment

---

## Enforcement

Community guidelines are enforced by the Nexment maintainers.

Depending on the severity of a violation, maintainers may:

1. Issue a warning.
2. Temporarily restrict participation.
3. Permanently remove access to the community.

Serious violations may result in immediate action without prior warning.

Maintainer decisions are final.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by emailing:

**support@nexment.in**

Reports will be reviewed as promptly and fairly as possible. We will make every reasonable effort to protect the privacy of those involved.

---

## Our Goal

Nexment exists to build software that is fast, simple, and privacy-first.

Our community should reflect the same values through respectful collaboration, thoughtful discussions, and a shared commitment to quality.
